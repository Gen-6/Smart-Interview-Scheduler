# Using Templates in Gen6

This short guide shows you how to import workflow templates into your Gen6 workspace.

## How to Use Templates

### 1. Click the **+** button in the Workflows section

In the **Workflows** panel on the left sidebar, click the **âž• (plus)** button.

![Step 1](https://gen6.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/step1.png)

---

### 2. Select **Import workflow**

From the menu that opens, click **Import workflow**.

![Step 2](https://gen6.ai/wp-content/uploads/2025/11/step2.png)

---

### 3. Choose your file

A file picker will appear.  
Select your exported workflow file, and it will be imported instantly into your workspace.
